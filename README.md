## 📖 Overview

YouTube Shorts excel at grabbing attention, while long-form videos drive deeper engagement and creator revenue. However, many users are unwilling to jump directly from a 30–60 second Short to a 20-minute video.

**YouTube Sparks** introduces a **3–8 minute mid-form video format** that bridges this gap by helping viewers transition from discovery to deeper engagement.

This project includes:

* 📄 A complete Product Requirements Document (PRD)
* 🔍 User research & competitive analysis
* 📊 Success metrics & analytics framework
* 🎨 Interactive mobile prototype
* 🚀 Go-to-Market strategy
* ⚠️ Risk assessment & trade-offs

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Users frequently discover interesting topics through Shorts but abandon the journey when asked to commit to a long-form video.

This creates a **"commitment cliff"** resulting in:

* Lower long-form watch time
* Lost creator revenue
* Reduced session depth
* Poor content discovery

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

**Sparks** is a new YouTube surface dedicated to **3–8 minute videos** that sits between Shorts and traditional long-form content.

### User Journey

```
Shorts
    ↓
Spark Recommendation
    ↓
3–8 Minute Spark
    ↓
Watch Full Video
```

This creates a gradual content journey rather than forcing users into long-form viewing.
